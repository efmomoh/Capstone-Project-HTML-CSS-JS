CREATIVE COMMONS GLOBAL SUMMIT 2023

A joyful celebration belieing in the value of openess and sharing, creating a positvie change with people over 80 countries is taking place in December 2023, in Liberia.

2023.15.12(THU) ~ (FRI)

@ National Museum of Liberia. Arts Center Monrovia and more.

Main Program

  • Lecture

    Listen to the speakers from various countries about the messages of sharing and opening.

  • CC Exibition

    Appreciate various creation appling CC License of Arists organized from Art Center Monrovia

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic

  • Workshop

    Try creating your own work using open source license rather than just watching

  • CC Ignite

    Get opportunities to study with CC affiliates around the world, also after the submit
